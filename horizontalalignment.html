Horizontal Alignment

Horizontal Alignment The text-align property sets the horizontal alignment (like left, right, or center) of the content in th or td. By default, the content of th elements are center-aligned and the content of td elements are left-aligned. to center-align the content of td elements as well, use text-align: center:
